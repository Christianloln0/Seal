About Seals

Seals are pinnipeds — marine mammals adapted for life in both land and sea. They evolved from land predators millions of years ago and became powerful ocean hunters.

🐟 Carnivorous marine hunters
❄️ Thick blubber for cold survival
🌊 Expert deep divers
🫧 Can slow heartbeat underwater
